Can I "restore" without losing my Jailbreak?

If you are on iOS 8.4 or below you may be able to perform a Semi Restore
For iDevices on iOS 5.0 - 8.4 you can use Semi Restore Tool by Coolstar
For iDevices on iOS 8.1 - 8.4 you can also use Cydia Impactor by Saurik

Should I Update from (iOS Version) to (Newer iOS Version)

Many People ask this question and the answer really is as simple as
If you care about Jailbreaking: Stay on the lowest version possible (even if not signed anymore)
If you care about your iDevices security: update to the newest iOS

I want to "Un-Jailbreak"

Going back to stock is easy!
To remove your Jailbreak, plug your iDevice into iTunes and backup (Optional) your phone.
Once it is finished backing up do a plain restore.
Once your iDevice has finished restoring it will be on stock iOS again!
If you wish to re-install the backup Go to File > Devices > Restore from Backup however its worth noting that backups of Jailbroken phones contains some remenants of tweaks and others stuff so if you want to avoid that not restoring the backup is your best option.
